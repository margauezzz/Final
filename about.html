HISTORY

In November 1985, when the Lechon Manok craze was sweeping the nation, Dwight and Dolores Salcedo pooled P5,000 from their savings and borrowed another P5,000 from their parents to put up their own kiosk. They named it Baliwag Lechon Manok, after Dolores’ hometown of Baliwag, Bulacan.

Over the years, they have added other well-loved items into their menu such as liempo and chicharon. They have also expanded their food business to include restaurants and fast food outlets.

The simple and grounded ways with which the couple has handled their business have allowed Baliwag to become one of the most iconic Filipino brand names nearly 25 years after it was started and, God willing, for decades more.

WHY "BALIWAG"?

Born and raised in Baliwag, Bulacan, Dolores believed that the mention of her hometown evoked memories of true Filipino food and hospitality. She said, “If the name can have that same effect on the ordinary Filipino, the business just might have a good chance of succeeding.”

VISION

Baliwag Lechon Manok hopes to maintain its status as a true household name, serving more people via accessible kiosks, outlets, and dine-in restaurants in major cities and other key locations worldwide.

MISSION

Baliwag Lechon Manok aims to provide easy access to its patrons by opening more outlets in strategic locations worldwide through company-led efforts and through franchising.

BALIWAG VALUES

Love for Family

It is, after all, rooted on the same values – its success made possible by its strong belief that the Family comes first, whether it be providing quality services to its treasured customers or dealing with members of its workforce.

Faith in the Company's Products and Services

It therefore believes that its continued success and growth depends entirely on ensuring the same quality food and hospitality Baliwag Lechon Manok is known for is maintained and enhanced through the years.

Professionalism &
Love for Work

All employees are trained and developed to be resourceful, honest and have genuine concern for both the company and its customers. The value of work – being given the opportunity to be gainfully employed and make an honest living – is also emphasized among all employees.

True Satisfaction

Knowing that its success was only made possible by providing the best food and best service possible, at very affordable prices, the company continues to strive for the same quality of food and service to satisfy its customers.
